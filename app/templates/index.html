Technology Adoption — All Archetypes

Shaded: 25th–75th percentile uncertainty band. Line: median (mean p, q). Normalized to % of TAM.

Bass Diffusion Parameters — All Archetypes

p = innovation coefficient (external influence), q = imitation coefficient (word-of-mouth / cost decline). Higher q → steeper S-curve. Peak adoption year is when the adoption rate is fastest.

Archetype p (innovation) q (imitation) q/p ratio Peak Year Inflection Year Maturity 25yr Penetration

Maturity Stage Definitions

Nascent — Very early; technology demonstrated but no commercial deployments at scale. High uncertainty.
Pre-Inflection — Early commercial activity; adoption accelerating but hasn't hit steep growth yet.
At Inflection — Entering the steepest part of the S-curve. Rapid scaling underway.
Early Growth — Past inflection, strong consistent growth, becoming mainstream.
Growth — Mature growth phase. Well-established, approaching saturation.
